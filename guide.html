No. The Claude / Cursor prompt in every report is written so AI coding tools can build most of the MVP for you. Plenty of subscribers are non-technical and ship with AI assistance alone.

Nothing is lost — Pro subscribers can always browse the full archive of past opportunities, and reports don't expire.

Yes, anytime, no contracts. You'll keep Pro access until the end of the period you already paid for.

Yes. Every opportunity is backed by real signals pulled from communities and forums, manually verified before publishing — not generated from nothing.
