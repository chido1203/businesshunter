The MVP — Exactly What To Build

3 screens. Nothing else.

  • Screen 1 — Provider setup (10 min): name, service type, hours, services offered.
  • Screen 2 — Public booking page: client picks a slot, enters name + phone, confirms. No account creation, no payment.
  • Screen 3 — Provider dashboard: this week's bookings, confirm/cancel.
  • Automatic SMS reminder 24h before each appointment via Twilio.

What NOT to build in v1: payments, video calls, staff management, reviews, marketing tools, analytics. Build only what removes the WhatsApp dependency. Ship in 4 weeks.

Validation Strategy — Before Writing Code

  1. Week 1 — Find 20 service providers on Instagram, Facebook groups, Reddit. Ask: "How do you currently manage your bookings?" Don't mention you're building anything.
  2. Week 2 — Qualify the pain. If the answer is more than 1 hour/week managing scheduling: you have a lead.
  3. Week 3 — Show a mockup. Ask: "Would you pay $9/month for this?"

Validation threshold: if 5 out of 20 say yes and give you their email — build it. Not 20 out of 20. Just 5.
