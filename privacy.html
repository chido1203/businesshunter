Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Business Hunter ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable law.

1. Who we are

Business Hunter is an independent publication and newsletter service. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at businesshunterservices@gmail.com.

2. What data we collect

We collect the following personal data:

  • Email address — when you subscribe to our free or paid newsletter
  • Payment information — processed by Lemon Squeezy (we never store card details)
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics (pages visited, time on site) via privacy-friendly tools
  • Cookies — minimal cookies for analytics and session management (see section 6)

3. How we use your data

  • To send you the newsletter you subscribed to
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To improve our content and understand what resonates with readers
  • To respond to your questions and support requests

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

  • Consent — when you submit your email via our sign-up form
  • Contract — to fulfil your paid subscription
  • Legitimate interest — for basic analytics to improve the product

5. Third-party services

We use the following services to operate Business Hunter:

  • Netlify — website hosting (US, EU data centres available)
  • Beehiiv — newsletter delivery and subscriber management
  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing and subscription management

Each of these services has its own privacy policy and acts as a data processor under GDPR.

6. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies to operate the site and optional analytics cookies (only with your consent). You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser cookies.

7. Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent at any time (by unsubscribing from emails)
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, email us at businesshunterservices@gmail.com.

8. Data retention

We retain your email address as long as you remain subscribed. When you unsubscribe, your data is deleted within 30 days. Payment records are kept for 7 years as required by law.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify subscribers by email before the changes take effect.
